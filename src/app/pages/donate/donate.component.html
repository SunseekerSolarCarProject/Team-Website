Making a donation

All monetary donations can be made through Western Michigan University's secure, online giving portal by choosing an amount. If you have any questions, or would like to donate something other than money, please let us know by sending an email to sunseeker@wmich.edu. Continue reading below for more information regarding the perks of being a sponsor!

Sponsorship Tiers

Platinum

Donations valued at no less than $10,000.

  • Logo will be placed on out current car. (1)
  • Larger area reserved on our car for the sponsor
  • Logo placed on website. (2)
  • Link to sponsor website (3)
  • A short description provided by the sponsor may be added to sponsor page (4)

Gold

Donations valued from $5,000 and up to $9,999.

  • Logo will be placed on out current car. (1)
  • Logo placed on website. (2)
  • Link to sponsor website (3)
  • A short description provided by the sponsor may be added to sponsor page (4)

Silver

Donations valued from $1,000 and up to $4,999.

  • Logo placed on website. (2)
  • Link to sponsor website (3)

Bronze

Donations valued at no more than $999.

  • Logo placed on website. (2)

Additional Information

All donations are welcome, if you are donating something besides or in addition to cash, we ask that you also provide a value for the goods and/or services. We will need a value to properly place you or your organization into a sponsorship tier. If you make multiple donations, the cumulative value will determine your sponsorship tier.

  1. Logo on exterior — The placement will be on the exterior of the current solar car. The size of the logo will be determined by sponsorship tier.
  2. Logo on website — Will be placed on the Our Sponsors page with company name and location. We ask that you provide the following: a logo, company name, and its location.
  3. Link to website — Links will be automatically be added for the sponsor unless specified otherwise.
  4. Sponsor description — A description of no more than 400 characters may be provided to be displaed on the Our Sponsors page next to your logo and company name.
